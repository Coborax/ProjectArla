Taking action for a better future

Our planet is facing a climate challenge. Farming and dairy carries a responsibility, but also a great opportunity to contribute positively and help solve that challenge. At Arla we want to be part of the solution.

Data-driven climate action

Arla farmers are already among the most climate-efficient dairy farmers in the world. But this is just the beginning.

We've created the world’s largest externally validated climate data sets from dairy farms to help our farmer owners become as climate efficient as possible.
